BarScribe Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 6, 2026

BarScribe ("we", "our", "the app") is a powerlifting training tracker built for lifters who value privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Data stored locally on your device:

  • Workout logs (exercises, sets, reps, weights, RPE)
  • Personal records (PRs and estimated 1RMs)
  • Training programs and meet preparation data
  • App settings (units, bodyweight, sex, rest timer preferences)
  • Profile photo (if you choose to add one for share cards)

All training data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. We do not have access to this data unless you explicitly enable cloud sync.

Data collected by third-party services (only when applicable):

  • RevenueCat — If you subscribe to BarScribe Pro, RevenueCat processes the transaction through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We receive your subscription status but not payment details. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase (optional) — If you enable cloud sync, your workout data is stored on Supabase servers. Your email address is used for authentication. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Expo — Expo may collect crash reports and anonymous usage analytics. See Expo's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the BarScribe training experience
  • To calculate PRs, training scores, and progress charts
  • To generate shareable workout cards (only when you tap "Share")
  • To manage your Pro subscription status
  • To sync data across devices (only if you enable cloud sync)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is only shared when:

  • You explicitly choose to share a workout card via the Share button
  • You enable cloud sync (data stored on Supabase)
  • Required by law or legal process

4. Photos and Camera

BarScribe requests camera and photo library access only to let you set a profile photo for workout share cards. Photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers unless you enable cloud sync.

5. Notifications

BarScribe uses local notifications for rest timer alerts during workouts. These notifications are processed entirely on your device and do not send data to external servers.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local data: Stored on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data.
  • Cloud data: If you enabled cloud sync, you can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us.
  • Subscription data: Managed by Apple/Google. Cancel through your device's subscription settings.

7. Children's Privacy

BarScribe is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

8. Security

Your local data is protected by your device's built-in security. Cloud-synced data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest on Supabase servers.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data (it's on your device)
  • Delete your data (uninstall the app or clear data)
  • Opt out of cloud sync at any time
  • Request deletion of cloud-stored data

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on this page with an updated effective date.

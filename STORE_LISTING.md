## Full Description

BarScribe is the powerlifting training tracker built by lifters, for lifters. Track your squat, bench, deadlift, and OHP with precision — no fluff, no distractions.

KEY FEATURES:

• LOG SETS WITH PRECISION — Weight, reps, and RPE for every set. Automatic PR detection using the Epley e1RM formula.

• SMART PROGRAMS — Run 5/3/1 (Wendler), Smolov Jr, or Texas Method with auto-generated workouts based on your training maxes.

• PERSONAL RECORDS — All-time best tracker across all four main lifts. See your progress over time with charts.

• MUSCLE HEATMAP — Visual breakdown of which muscles you hit each session. Front and back body view with color-coded intensity.

• WORKOUT SCORING — Get a 0-100 workout score based on volume load, INOL, muscle coverage, and PR bonuses. Know exactly how productive your session was.

• SHAREABLE WORKOUT CARDS — Generate branded workout summary cards for Instagram, Twitter, and more. Show off your gains.

• MEET PREP — Plan your powerlifting meet. Set openers, calculate warmups, and manage all three attempts for SBD.

• CALCULATORS — One-rep max (Epley), Wilks score, and DOTS score calculators. Compare yourself across weight classes.

• TRAINING GUIDE — Built-in reference for RPE, INOL, 1RM formulas, program details, and scoring methodology.

• HISTORY & CALENDAR — Browse past workouts by date. Review every session in detail.

BARSCRIBE PRO:
• Progress charts and PR trends
• Advanced INOL breakdown
• Meet preparation tools
• Wilks & DOTS calculators
• 7-day free trial

Built for serious lifters. No ads. No social media noise. Just iron.
